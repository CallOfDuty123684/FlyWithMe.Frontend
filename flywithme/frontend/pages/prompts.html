Prompt Guidelines for Fly With Me

How to Prompt Properly:

Effective prompts ensure our Fly With Me website processes your requests accurately and efficiently. Follow these guidelines:

  • Use Clear Language: Describe your travel preferences clearly in proper English.
  • Include Required Details: Provide the number of days, desired locations, and any specific preferences (like adventure type).
  • Be Concise: Avoid unnecessary details or ambiguous requests.
  • Avoid Jargon: Use language understandable by all users, regardless of their travel expertise.
  • Format Example: "Please suggest a 5-day itinerary for Paris, France, focusing on cultural sites."

By following these guidelines, you help us tailor the perfect travel itinerary for your needs.
